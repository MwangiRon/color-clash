How to Play

üéØ Objective

Get 4 pieces in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) before your opponent does!

üéÆ Gameplay

  • The game is played on a 4x4 grid (16 cells)
  • Red player goes first, then players alternate turns
  • Click an empty cell to place your piece
  • First player to get 4 in a row wins!

‚ö° Power Move

Each player has ONE power move per game:

  • Toggle "Use Power Move" checkbox
  • Click an opponent's piece to flip it to your color
  • Use it strategically - you only get one!
